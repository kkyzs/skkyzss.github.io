6th June 2019 - "Goodbye"

This will be the last entry in this diary. I got rid of everything. Trashed the pill and burned the research papers, there's no going back now, and I'll eventually face the same fate that Algernon did, but I have no regrets. I put my name down for residence at a state-sponsored home for the mentally handicapped. They were confused as to why I want to be admitted in there, but I guess that in itself is an anomaly that was enough to get my accepted. Why? I can't stand the thought of being pitied by my friends and coworkers. I guess I still have some pride left despite my intelligence generally regressing. It's been a long year.

To the person that finds this diary, all I wish is for you to lay flowers for Algernon once in awhile.

"He was a dear friend of mine, my only friend."
